A train leaves city A heading toward city B at 60 mph. At the same moment, another train leaves city B (300 miles away) heading toward city A at 40 mph. Work through, step by step, exactly when and where the two trains meet. Show every step of the reasoning. Then generalize: derive a formula for the meeting time and meeting distance from A for arbitrary speeds v1 and v2 over an arbitrary separation d, and explain why the formula makes intuitive sense.
